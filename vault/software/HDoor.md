## S0061

[HDoor](https://attack.mitre.org/software/S0061) is malware that has been customized and used by the [Naikon](https://attack.mitre.org/groups/G0019) group. [^1] 

### Techniques Used
| ID | Name | Use |
| --- | --- | --- |
| [[Network Service Discovery\|T1046]] | Network Service Discovery | [HDoor](https://attack.mitre.org/software/S0061) scans to identify open ports on the victim.[^1]  |
| [[Disable or Modify Tools\|T1685]] | Disable or Modify Tools | [HDoor](https://attack.mitre.org/software/S0061) kills anti-virus found on the victim.[^1]  |
