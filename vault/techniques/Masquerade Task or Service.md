## T1036.004

Adversaries may attempt to manipulate the name of a task or service to make it appear legitimate or benign. Tasks/services executed by the Task Scheduler or systemd will typically be given a name and/or description.[^394] [^258]  Windows services will have a service name as well as a display name. Many benign tasks and services exist that have commonly associated names. Adversaries may give tasks or services names that are similar or identical to those of legitimate ones.<br><br>Tasks or services contain other fields, such as a description, that adversaries may attempt to make appear legitimate.[^223] [^308]
