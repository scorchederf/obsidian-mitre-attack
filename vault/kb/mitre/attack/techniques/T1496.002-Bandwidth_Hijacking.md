## Description

Adversaries may leverage the network bandwidth resources of co-opted systems to complete resource-intensive tasks, which may impact system and/or hosted service availability. <br><br>Adversaries may also use malware that leverages a system's network bandwidth as part of a botnet in order to facilitate [[kb/mitre/attack/techniques/T1498-Network_Denial_of_Service\|Network Denial of Service]] campaigns and/or to seed malicious torrents.[^1]  Alternatively, they may engage in proxyjacking by selling use of the victims' network bandwidth and IP address to proxyware services.[^2]  Finally, they may engage in internet-wide scanning in order to identify additional targets for compromise.[^3] <br><br>In addition to incurring potential financial costs or availability disruptions, this technique may cause reputational damage if a victim’s bandwidth is used for illegal activities.[^2]
