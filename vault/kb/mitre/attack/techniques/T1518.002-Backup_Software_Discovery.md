## Description

Adversaries may attempt to get a listing of backup software or configurations that are installed on a system. Adversaries may use this information to shape follow-on behaviors, such as [[kb/mitre/attack/techniques/T1485-Data_Destruction\|Data Destruction]], [[kb/mitre/attack/techniques/T1490-Inhibit_System_Recovery\|Inhibit System Recovery]], or [[kb/mitre/attack/techniques/T1486-Data_Encrypted_for_Impact\|Data Encrypted for Impact]].  <br><br>Commands that can be used to obtain security software information are [[kb/mitre/attack/software/S0108-netsh\|netsh]], `reg query` with [[kb/mitre/attack/software/S0075-Reg\|Reg]], `dir` with [[kb/mitre/attack/software/S0106-cmd\|cmd]], and [[kb/mitre/attack/software/S0057-Tasklist\|Tasklist]], but other indicators of discovery behavior may be more specific to the type of software or security system the adversary is looking for, such as Veeam, Acronis, Dropbox, or Paragon.[^1]
