## Description

Adversaries may create email accounts that can be used during targeting. Adversaries can use accounts created with email providers to further their operations, such as leveraging them to conduct [[kb/mitre/attack/techniques/T1598-Phishing_for_Information\|Phishing for Information]] or [[kb/mitre/attack/techniques/T1566-Phishing\|Phishing]].[^2]  Establishing email accounts may also allow adversaries to abuse free services – such as trial periods – to [[kb/mitre/attack/techniques/T1583-Acquire_Infrastructure\|Acquire Infrastructure]] for follow-on purposes.[^3] <br><br>Adversaries may also take steps to cultivate a persona around the email account, such as through use of [[kb/mitre/attack/techniques/T1585.001-Social_Media_Accounts\|Social Media Accounts]], to increase the chance of success of follow-on behaviors. Created email accounts can also be used in the acquisition of infrastructure (ex: [[kb/mitre/attack/techniques/T1583.001-Domains\|Domains]]).[^2] <br><br>To decrease the chance of physically tying back operations to themselves, adversaries may make use of disposable email services.[^1]
