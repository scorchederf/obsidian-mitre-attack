## Description

Adversaries may abuse odbcconf.exe to proxy execution of malicious payloads. Odbcconf.exe is a Windows utility that allows you to configure Open Database Connectivity (ODBC) drivers and data source names.[^2]  The Odbcconf.exe binary may be digitally signed by Microsoft.<br><br>Adversaries may abuse odbcconf.exe to bypass application control solutions that do not account for its potential abuse. Similar to [[kb/mitre/attack/techniques/T1218.010-Regsvr32\|Regsvr32]], odbcconf.exe has a `REGSVR` flag that can be misused to execute DLLs (ex: `odbcconf.exe /S /A &lbrace;REGSVR "C:\Users\Public\file.dll"&rbrace;`). [^3] [^1] [^4]  <br>
