## G0033

[Poseidon Group](https://attack.mitre.org/groups/G0033) is a Portuguese-speaking threat group that has been active since at least 2005. The group has a history of using information exfiltrated from victims to blackmail victim companies into contracting the [Poseidon Group](https://attack.mitre.org/groups/G0033) as a security firm. [^1]

| --- | --- | --- |
| [[System Network Connections Discovery\|T1049]] | System Network Connections Discovery | [Poseidon Group](https://attack.mitre.org/groups/G0033) obtains and saves information about victim network interfaces and addresses.[^1]  |
| [[OS Credential Dumping\|T1003]] | OS Credential Dumping | [Poseidon Group](https://attack.mitre.org/groups/G0033) conducts credential dumping on victims, with a focus on obtaining credentials belonging to domain and database servers.[^1]  |
