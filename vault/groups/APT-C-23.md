## G1028

[APT-C-23](https://attack.mitre.org/groups/G1028) is a threat group that has been active since at least 2014.[^6]  [APT-C-23](https://attack.mitre.org/groups/G1028) has primarily focused its operations on the Middle East, including Israeli military assets. [APT-C-23](https://attack.mitre.org/groups/G1028) has developed mobile spyware targeting Android and iOS devices since 2017.[^4]
